Process

Website Design process that
covers your unique business goals

1. Initial Consultation

To discuss your project in more detail. Team trueestudio provides a discussion service through online meetings that will help you gather additional information about your project and clarify things that are not yet clear.

2. Proposal & price quotation

Based on your project needs, trueestudio will prepare a proposal and price quote. The proposal will contain the scope of work, timeline, and associated costs.

3. Creation of initial design / prototype

We will create an initial design / prototype of the appearance and flow based on the project details that you have described earlier.

4. Implementation of work

After approval and full payment, Trueestudio will begin work on your project. And we will update the progress of your project every week.
